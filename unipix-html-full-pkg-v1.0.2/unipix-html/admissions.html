Admission Process

Your Journey to Plato’s Academy Begins Here

  • Visit the school or contact us via phone/website for admission enquiries.
  • Collect the admission form from the office or download it online.
  • Fill in the form carefully with accurate student and parent details.
  • Submit the completed form along with required documents.
  • Attach a copy of the birth certificate and recent passport size photographs.
  • Provide previous school report cards and transfer certificate (if applicable).
  • Submit Aadhaar copies of the student and parents/guardians.
  • Attend a campus tour to understand our environment and facilities.
  • Students from Grade 1 and above will attend a basic entrance test.
  • Kindergarten applicants will have an informal interaction with faculty.
  • Shortlisted students will receive admission confirmation from the school.
  • Pay the admission and term fees within the specified time.
  • Collect the admission receipt and confirmation letter.
  • Attend the parent-student orientation before the academic year begins.

Admission Enquiry

At Plato’s Academy, Tirupur, we welcome curious minds and committed learners to be part of an educational journey that shapes character, sharpens intellect, and nurtures global perspectives. Whether you are seeking a strong academic foundation, competitive exam preparation, or holistic development for your child, our admission team is here to guide you every step of the way. We believe in creating a smooth, transparent admission process where parents and students feel informed, supported, and confident.

If you’re interested in enrolling your child at Plato’s Academy, we encourage you to reach out through our enquiry form, visit our campus, or connect with our admissions office. Our team is happy to provide detailed information about curriculum options, grade availability, entrance assessments, and campus life.
