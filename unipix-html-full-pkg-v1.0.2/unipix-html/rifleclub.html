About Platos Rifle Club Association

Plato’s Rifle Club is a premier training hub affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Shooting Association and the National Rifle Association of India. Designed to international standards, the club offers structured coaching in rifle and pistol shooting under the guidance of certified experts.

VISION & MISSION

Focused on precision, discipline, and performance, the club prepares students for competitive success at the state, national, and international levels. With a strong emphasis on mental fortitude and technical mastery, Plato’s Rifle Club is where young marksmen evolve into champions — calm, focused, and future-ready.

Our History

It was founded in 2020 with a view to promote and popularize the shooting sports in the district of Tiruppur.

Winning History

Our Student Tanniska Senthilkumar has won the Silver Medal 🥈 in Women’s Trap Shooting at Khelo India 2025!
